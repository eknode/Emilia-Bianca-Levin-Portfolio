About

Emilia is a New York-based artist whose work centers on oil painting while spanning multiple disciplines. Her work delves into the intersections of texture, color, and form, creating art narratives that balance abstraction and realism.

Currently pursuing a BFA in Fine Art at Parsons School of Design, Emilia’s artistic approach is rooted in observation and experimentation. She draws inspiration from her identity, memory, and the natural world, often blending traditional painting techniques with contemporary methods.
